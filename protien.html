Delicious Protein Recipes

Grilled Chicken Breast

Marinate chicken breast with olive oil, garlic, lemon juice, and herbs. Grill on medium heat for 6-7 minutes per side.

Pan-Seared Salmon

Season salmon with salt, pepper, and paprika. Sear in a pan with butter for 4-5 minutes per side until crispy.

High-Protein Egg Bowl

Scramble eggs with spinach, mushrooms, and feta cheese for a healthy protein-packed breakfast.

Tofu Stir Fry

Saute tofu with soy sauce, ginger, and veggies for a delicious plant-based protein dish.

Spicy Lentil Soup

Cook lentils with tomatoes, garlic, and cumin for a comforting, high-protein soup.

Baked Turkey Meatballs

Mix ground turkey with herbs and breadcrumbs, then bake at 375°F for 20 minutes.

Greek Yogurt with Nuts

Mix Greek yogurt with almonds, walnuts, and a drizzle of honey for a protein-rich snack.

Garlic Butter Shrimp

Sauté shrimp in garlic butter for a quick and high-protein seafood dish.

Quinoa & Black Bean Bowl

Cook quinoa and mix it with black beans, corn, and avocado for a nutritious meal.

Cottage Cheese Delight

Top cottage cheese with fresh fruits and a sprinkle of cinnamon for a tasty protein snack.
