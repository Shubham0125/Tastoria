Delicious Air Fryer Recipes

Air Fryer Chicken Breast

Season chicken breast and air fry at 375°F for 15-18 minutes, flipping halfway.

Air Fryer French Fries

Cut potatoes into fries, season, and air fry at 400°F for 15 minutes.

Air Fryer Salmon

Season salmon and air fry at 375°F for 10-12 minutes for a crispy outer layer.

Air Fryer Onion Rings

Coat onion slices in batter and air fry at 375°F for 10 minutes.

Air Fryer Chicken Wings

Season wings and air fry at 380°F for 25 minutes, flipping halfway.

Air Fryer Mozzarella Sticks

Coat mozzarella sticks in breadcrumbs and air fry at 375°F for 6 minutes.

Air Fryer Sweet Potato Fries

Cut sweet potatoes into fries, season, and air fry at 400°F for 12 minutes.

Air Fryer Garlic Shrimp

Sauté shrimp in garlic butter and air fry at 375°F for 8 minutes.

Air Fryer Crispy Bacon

Lay bacon strips in the air fryer and cook at 350°F for 8-10 minutes.

Air Fryer Donuts

Use biscuit dough, air fry at 350°F for 6 minutes, and coat with sugar.
